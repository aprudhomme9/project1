The rules of BlackJack, or 21, are as follows:

The name of the game is 21 -- your goal is to get a hand as close to 21 as possible without going over.
First, place a bet by clicking on the chips. You can bet as much as is in your bank.
Once you place your bet, your cards will be flipped, and you can HIT or STAY.
If you hit, another card will be dealt into your hand.
If you stay, the dealer will flip his card over, and your hands will be compared
COUNT: You'll see a "count" in the top left corner. This signifies whether the deck is heavy or light
A heavy deck is represented by a positive count, and means you're more likely to draw high-value cards.
A light deck is just the opposite, and is represented by a negative count.
It is recommended to be more aggressive with a heavy deck than a light deck -- though there are many strategies.
This is simply meant to help you in learning the game and becoming a more strategic player!
